Teamwork is something I'm familiar with since university, since in the various courses I had to contribute, direct, motivate and collaborate with my other co-workers, essentially when it came to reaching a complicated goal. For me it is important that all team members feel comfortable within the creative environment and thus achieve a productive synergy when it comes to fulfilling the tasks that were assigned.

Acceptance of criticism

In projects it is normal to make mistakes, which is why it is common for teammates or superiors to have comments for improvement towards the work done, for which it is important for me to have a good attitude towards feedback, without taking negative attitudes and understanding that They are opportunities for improvement in the various weak points we have.

Troubleshooting

Difficulties appear unexpectedly, which is why I think it is important to know how to handle complicated circumstances correctly, regardless of the scenario they arise. I consider it important to remain calm in order to think of a prompt and effective solution so as not to allow problems to escalate.

Critical thinking

One of the most important skills for me is the ability to properly criticize what we do every day, avoiding being biased in that everything we do is done correctly and thus giving way to a reflection based on arguments. Time management

For me, both personal and professional organization is of the utmost importance. Knowing how long a task can take is essential to be able to deliver the tasks in the corresponding times or ask for a little help and time from more experienced colleagues.
